About

Academic Focus

Research centered on behavioral modeling and multi-method analytics in aviation.

Mahmut Bakır graduated from the Department of Civil Air Transport Management at Mustafa Kemal University, completed his master's and Ph.D. in Civil Aviation Management at Anadolu University, and currently serves as an associate professor at Samsun University.

His main research focus is behavioral modeling and multi-method analytics in aviation. His work also covers airline business, service marketing, customer experience, sustainable aviation, bibliometric mapping, and multi-criteria decision approaches.
